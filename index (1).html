WeChat Commerce · Sephora China

Sephora China — WeChat Livestream Commerce Platform

When Tencent opened WeChat to livestream commerce in 2019, our team was first to implement it at luxury retail scale for Sephora China — pioneering a format the entire cosmetics industry subsequently followed.

80+ live broadcasts in the first two months. 3.5 million cumulative viewers by end of 2020. 230,000 viewers for a single Estée Lauder Double 11 event. WeChat's contribution to total Sephora China sales grew 2.35× year-on-year.

★ MMA Smarties APAC 2020 — Gold
Global Rollout · DFS Worldwide · LVMH

La Samaritaine — Digital Brand Platform, 20 Markets

La Samaritaine reopened in June 2021 after a €750 million renovation — one of the most significant retail relaunches in Europe in decades, managed by DFS under LVMH.

We built the digital brand portal that underpins La Samaritaine's global content and marketing operations, then deployed it across 20 DFS locations worldwide — each adapted to its local market — in under 12 months.

20-country rollout · 12 months · LVMH standards
Luxury Membership · Fine Wine · Paris

FICOFI — Le Club Members Digital Platform

Founded in Bordeaux in 1990, FICOFI is one of the most discreet and prestigious fine wine clubs in the world. Its 300 Members — drawn from finance, industry, and the arts — have direct access to estates that don't advertise: Château Lafite, DRC, Petrus, and beyond.

We built the digital membership platform and app: cellar management, exclusive event access, in-depth editorial content, direct lines to the châteaux. When 300 Members expect nothing less than perfect, the digital experience has to match.
